When to use this page

Choose this weekly timesheet calculator when you want one full-week total instead of calculating each shift separately.

  • Leave unused days blank.
  • Overnight rows are supported.
  • Switch to Timecard for more flexible rows.
FAQ

Weekly timesheet questions

Use these answers to help visitors understand how to total a week and where overtime fits into the calculation.

Do I need to fill every day?

No. You can leave unused days blank and the calculator will skip them.

What if one day crosses midnight?

The script detects when the end time is earlier than the start time and treats that row as an overnight shift.

How is overtime shown?

The page compares total weekly minutes against your threshold and shows how many minutes remain regular versus overtime.

Can I check one day only?

Yes, but for a faster one-shift calculation the timesheet calculator with breaks may be a better fit.
