Homepage FAQ

Frequently asked questions

These quick answers cover the most common questions about using the calculators and reading your totals.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Just open the calculator you need, enter your times, and review the result.

Can the calculators handle overnight shifts?

Yes. If the end time is earlier than the start time, the calculator treats the shift as crossing midnight.

Can I include breaks and minutes?

Yes. The timesheet, weekly, and timecard tools let you subtract break minutes and show clear totals.

Which page should I use first?

Start with Timesheet for a single shift, Weekly for a full week, or Timecard for multiple date rows.
