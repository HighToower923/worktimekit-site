FAQ

Questions about time differences

Many users search for an hours between times calculator to avoid manual counting. These answers explain what the tool does.

Does this subtract lunch or breaks?

No. This page only measures raw time between the start and end points. Use the timesheet calculator if you need break deductions.

Can it calculate overnight durations?

Yes. A range such as 11:30 PM to 4:15 AM is treated as crossing midnight.

Why show decimal hours?

Decimal values are easier to transfer into payroll sheets and productivity logs, while the hours-and-minutes view is easier to read at a glance.

What tool should I use for a whole week?

Move to the weekly timesheet calculator when you need a Monday through Sunday total.
