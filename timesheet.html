FAQ

Questions about this timesheet calculator

These answers cover the most common issues users run into when they total shift hours and break deductions.

How does the break deduction work?

The calculator subtracts the break minutes from the total shift length, so the result shows paid time instead of total time on site.

Can I use it for overnight shifts?

Yes. A shift like 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM is handled automatically as an overnight shift.

Why show decimal hours too?

Payroll systems often want decimal totals such as 7.50 hours, while employees may prefer hours and minutes. This page gives both.

Where should I go for a whole week?

Use the weekly timesheet calculator when you need Monday through Sunday totals instead of a single shift.
