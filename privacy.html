Privacy Notes

Privacy policy

This page explains how WorkTimeKit handles calculator entries, how Google Analytics is used on the site, and what basic cookie-related information visitors should know.

Information you enter

The calculators run in your browser and no account is required to use them. The calculator pages themselves do not submit or store the entries you type when you total work hours, breaks, overtime, or timecard rows.

This means your shift times and calculator results stay in your browser while you use the site unless you choose to contact us separately by email.

Analytics and cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics may collect anonymized data such as pages visited, time on page, and general location (country level). Cookies may be used to support analytics and improve user experience.

Analytics information helps us understand which pages people use most often and which calculators are useful to visitors.

Third-party services

The main third-party service currently used on the site is Google Analytics. Google may process limited usage data according to its own policies in order to provide analytics reports for site owners.

If additional services are added in the future, this page can be updated so visitors can review any important privacy changes in one place.

Your choices

You can stop using the calculators at any time if you do not want to enter work-hour details. You can also adjust your browser settings if you want more control over cookies and site data.

For questions about privacy or site policies, use the contact page or email hello@worktimekit.com. Return to the homepage or explore the FAQ page for more information about the calculator tools.

FAQ

Privacy questions

These short answers cover the most common privacy questions.

Does WorkTimeKit store my calculator entries?

No. The calculators run in your browser, no account is required, and the calculator pages themselves do not submit or store the time entries you type.

Does WorkTimeKit use Google Analytics?

Yes. We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics may collect anonymized data such as pages visited, time on page, and general location at the country level.

Are cookies used on the site?

Yes. Cookies may be used to support Google Analytics and improve user experience while you browse the site.

Where can I ask a privacy question?

If you have a question about privacy or site policies, use the contact page and email hello@worktimekit.com.
