Privacy Notes

Privacy policy

This privacy page explains how the static version of WorkTimeKit handles calculator input, browser-only processing, and placeholder areas that may later be connected to analytics or advertising services.

Top Ad PlacementReserved for an ad unit on the privacy page.

Local calculations

All calculator math on the current site runs inside the browser using JavaScript. No shift details, start times, break minutes, or timecard rows are sent to a server in this static version.

If you later connect the site to analytics, form processing, or advertising platforms, update this page to reflect what information those services collect.

Cookies and advertising

The site currently includes only ad placeholders. If AdSense or another ad network is added later, that provider may use cookies or related technologies to personalize and measure ads.

When you publish the final version, review the ad network requirements and add any consent messaging needed for your audience.

Third-party services

If you connect the contact page to a static form provider or add visitor analytics, those third parties may collect basic technical information. You should document the provider names, data they process, and any opt-out options here.

For now, the site is a front-end project with no bundled analytics scripts and no remote API calls.

Your choices

Because the calculators work locally, visitors can simply close the browser tab if they do not want to continue using the tools. For questions about privacy changes or future policies, use the contact page.

Return to the homepage or explore the FAQ page for more information about the calculator tools.

FAQ

Privacy questions

These short answers clarify how privacy works in the static version of the website.

Does WorkTimeKit store my time entries?

No. In this version, time entries are processed in the browser and are not saved to a database.

Are cookies active now?

Not by default in this project. Cookie use would depend on any analytics or ad platform you add during deployment.

Will this page need updates later?

Yes, especially if you connect forms, analytics, consent banners, or AdSense scripts to the published site.

Where can I learn what the calculators do?

Visit the about page, the FAQ page, or one of the calculator pages such as Timesheet.
