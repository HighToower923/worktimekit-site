Privacy Notes

Privacy policy

This page explains how WorkTimeKit handles the information you enter while using the calculators and what may change if additional site tools are added later.

Information you enter

The calculators run in your browser and no account is required to use them. The calculator pages themselves do not submit or store the entries you type when you total work hours, breaks, or overtime.

If contact, analytics, or other site tools are added later, this page may be updated to explain what information they use and why.

Cookies and advertising

The current calculator pages do not rely on advertising or analytics tools to run. If those services are added later, they may use cookies or similar technologies.

If that happens, this policy may be updated with the details visitors need to know.

Third-party services

If a contact tool, analytics service, or another third-party feature is added later, those providers may collect limited information needed to run their services.

Any such services should be listed here along with links to their own privacy policies.

Your choices

You can stop using the calculators at any time if you do not want to enter time details. For questions about privacy or policy changes, use the contact page.

Return to the homepage or explore the FAQ page for more information about the calculator tools.

FAQ

Privacy questions

These short answers cover the most common privacy questions.

Does WorkTimeKit store my time entries?

No. No account is required, and the calculator pages themselves do not store the time entries you type.

Are cookies active now?

Any cookie use would depend on services that may be added later, such as advertising or measurement tools.

Will this page need updates later?

Yes. It may be updated if the site adds new services, contact options, or advertising features.

Where can I learn what the calculators do?

Visit the about page, the FAQ page, or one of the calculator pages such as Timesheet.
