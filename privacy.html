Privacy Notes

Privacy policy

This page explains how WorkTimeKit handles the information you enter while using the calculators.

Information you enter

The main calculators are designed to help you total work hours, breaks, and overtime without asking you to create an account. Your entries are not submitted through the calculators themselves.

If contact or measurement tools are added later, this page can be updated to explain what they collect and how they are used.

Cookies and advertising

Advertising or measurement services may use cookies or similar tools to understand visits and improve the experience.

If those services are added, this page should be updated with the details visitors need to know.

Third-party services

If a contact tool, analytics service, or other third-party feature is added, those providers may collect limited information needed to run their services.

Any such services should be listed here along with links to their own privacy policies.

Your choices

You can stop using the calculators at any time if you do not want to enter time details. For questions about privacy or policy changes, use the contact page.

Return to the homepage or explore the FAQ page for more information about the calculator tools.

FAQ

Privacy questions

These short answers cover the most common privacy questions.

Does WorkTimeKit store my time entries?

No. The calculator pages do not ask you to create an account or store your time entries.

Are cookies active now?

Cookie use depends on any services added to the site, such as advertising or measurement tools.

Will this page need updates later?

Yes. It may be updated if the site adds new services, contact options, or advertising features.

Where can I learn what the calculators do?

Visit the about page, the FAQ page, or one of the calculator pages such as Timesheet.
